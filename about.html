About

PAIWT [PAWNT] is the world’s first art game using cryptocurrency to create a market of blocks revolvIng around unreplicatable art designs. PAIWT is an exciting art creation game using the Crosswaters™️ method to ultimately generate cryptocurrency. Players create Unique PAIWT Explosions (UPEs) according to the game rules and then submit scans of their work for cryptocurrency rewards and bonuses. Individual UPEs will have a distinct visual appearance determined by the sequence of colors players choose during the game. After UPEs become part of a block, they can be bought, sold, or transferred digitally. All the block digital UPEs are unique and 100% owned and controlled by the artists; they cannot be replicated, stolen, or destroyed. UPEs are not a cryptocurrency; they’re a cryptocollectible.

Each game of Paiwt comes with 5 random Ernie Paiwt-Hands. Each Ernie has a unique code that enables you 10 Blocks, giving you 50 total blocks every game.
