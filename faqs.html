FAQs

What is Paiwt?

Paiwt is an art creation game using the Crosswaters™️ method to ultimately generate cryptocurrency. Players create Unique Paiwt Explosions™ (UPE) according to the game rules and then submit scans of their work for cryptocurrency rewards and bonuses.

What do I need to play Paiwt?

Everything you need to play comes with Paiwt - The Art Creation Game:

Paiwt Board

Paiwt Cards

Paiwt Game Crosswaters™ Colored Medium Set

Paiwt Game Crosswaters™ Colors Set

Paint Brush

Straw

Five Ernie Paiwt-Hands

What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency (or crypto currency) is a digital asset designed to work as a medium of exchange that uses cryptography to secure its transactions, to control the creation of additional units, and to verify the transfer of assets.

Can I acquire cryptocurrency from Paiwt?

Yes

What is an Ernie Paiwt-Hand?

An Ernie Paiwt,-Hand is a game piece that contains cryptocurrency information and a unique code used for block entry and subsequent randomization into the bonuses schedule.

What is a Cryptocollectable?

The real-world analogy for a cryptocurrency is dollars or pounds; a cryptocollectible’s real world analogy is closer to assets like baseball cards or fine art.

What is a Unique Paiwt Explosion™?

A Unique Paiwt Explosion™ (UPE) is the final scannable art creation that is peeled off of the game board surface after the game is over. Its approximate diameter is about 3 inches. Explosions are unique because Crosswaters ™ media, colors, and methodology are used.

What are Crosswaters™ media?

Crosswaters™ media is a specially formulated water soluble non toxic mixture consisting of a binder mixed with water, chalk, gypsum, and colored dye. Paiwt contains seven different colors of Crosswaters™ media.

What are Crosswaters™ colors ?

Crosswaters™ colors are specially formulated water soluble non toxic dyes. They are essential, along with Crosswaters™ media, to produce the unique fractal effect. Paiwt contains seven different Crosswaters™ colors.

How is every UPE unique?

The Crosswaters™ method️ creates unique fractal designs with infinite possibilities. The Paiwt rules add another layer of complexity that further guarantees irreproducibility.

Why are Paiwt cryptocollectables valuable?

Paiwt uses block technology to store various cryptocurrencys on handmade unique art creations known as Unique Paiwt Explosions.

How are UPE prices calculated?

Each approved UPE starts with 2(USD) of your Ernie-Paiwt Hands cryptocurrency, and start with a standard selling price of 5(USD) of that cryptocurrency.

How are UPE sold?

Buyers take part in a silent auction by bidding on pieces once a month starting at 5(USD) of the Unique Paiwt Explosions assigned cryptocurrency.

Are UPE like Bitcoin?

Unique Paiwt Explosions are NOT a cryptocurrency. They’re more like a cryptocollectible. The real-world analogy for a cryptocurrency is dollars or pounds; a cryptocollectible’s real world analogy is closer to assets like baseball cards or fine art. Any UPE you own belongs to you.
